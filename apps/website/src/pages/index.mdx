## Features

- **Open Source**: StoryBooker is an open-source project, allowing you to customize and extend its functionality to suit your needs.
- **Self-Hosted**: You have full control over your Storybook instances by hosting them on your own infrastructure.
- **Easy Deployment**: Deploy StoryBooker on popular cloud platforms like Azure, AWS, and GCP with minimal setup.
- **User Management**: Manage users and permissions to control access to your Storybook instances.
- **Multiple Storybooks**: Host and manage multiple Storybook instances from a single interface.
- **Custom adapters**: Use custom adapters to connect to different storage solutions or services.
- **OpenAPI Spec**: StoryBooker provides an OpenAPI specification for easy integration with other tools and services.
